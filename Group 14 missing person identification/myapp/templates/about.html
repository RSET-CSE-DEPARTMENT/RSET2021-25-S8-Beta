About Us



Our Mission

At Detectify, we harness the power of **AI-driven face recognition** and **real-time YOLO-based surveillance** to help identify missing persons. Our system analyzes **CCTV footage**, detects faces, and matches them against a reference database, enabling law enforcement and organizations to track individuals efficiently.

We aim to make **public safety proactive** by **reducing response time** in missing person cases. Our advanced deep learning models process large-scale video feeds to **detect, verify, and alert authorities in real-time**.

How It Works

Our missing person identification system follows a structured approach:

  • Face Detection: Using **YOLOv8 (face.pt)**, the system detects faces in CCTV footage.
  • Feature Extraction: **DeepFace** extracts facial embeddings for unique identification.
  • Face Matching: Facial embeddings are compared against an uploaded reference image.

Why Choose Detectify?

  • Real-Time Processing: High-speed AI models analyze video streams in **real-time**.
  • Privacy-First Approach: No permanent image storage, ensuring **data security**.
  • Seamless Integration: Works with **existing CCTV systems** for easy deployment.
  • Scalable & Flexible: Can be adapted for use in **airports, schools, hospitals, and public spaces**.
